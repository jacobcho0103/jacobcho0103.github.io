Young-ho Cho

I am Young-ho Cho, a Ph.D. candidate in theChandra Family Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at The University of Texas at Austin, advised by Prof. Hao Zhu.

My research develops data-driven modeling for power system operation. I am especially interested in emerging energy challenges driven by the rapid growth of artificial intelligence. My research focuses on the co-optimization of energy systems for serving AI workloads (Energy4AI) and the application of AI to improve the operation, planning, and resilience of energy systems (AI4Energy).

I earned my B.S. in Electrical and Electronics Engineering (Minor in Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering) from Konkuk University under the supervision of Prof. Duehee Lee.
